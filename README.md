# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built using the **Sample Superstore** dataset to analyze sales, profit, customer segments, product categories, and regional performance. The dashboard provides business insights through dynamic KPIs, maps, charts, and slicers to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard offers a comprehensive view of sales performance across different dimensions, including **time, region, category, segment, and shipping mode**. Users can interact with filters to explore trends, identify profitable products, and understand regional business performance.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The project uses the **Sample Superstore** dataset.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop**
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* CSV Dataset

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* 💰 Total Sales
* 📈 Total Profit
* 📊 Profit Margin
* 🛒 Total Orders
* 💵 Average Order Value

### Interactive Filters

* Year
* Region
* Category
* Segment
* Ship Mode

### Visualizations

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales & Profit by Category
* Sales by Customer Segment
* Profit by Sub-Category
* State-wise Sales Map
* Business Insights Panel

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

* **Technology** is the most profitable product category despite lower sales volume.
* **Consumer** segment contributes the highest share of total sales.
* **West** region generates the highest revenue, with California leading sales.
* **Tables** sub-category records losses, while **Copiers** generate the highest profit.
* Higher discounts negatively impact overall profitability.
* Sales peak during **November and December**, indicating strong seasonal demand.
